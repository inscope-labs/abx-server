Privacy Policy

ABX — Diagnostic & Control Android Application
Last Updated: July 14, 2026

ABX is committed to absolute user privacy. This application operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, process, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how ABX (the "Application"), a diagnostic and control Android application, handles information. Unlike typical mobile applications, ABX is designed to run completely offline and locally on your mobile device. It does not require user accounts, cloud database registration, or tracking SDKs.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII). This means:

  • No Identifiers: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or hardware identifiers.
  • No Accounts: There is no registration system, login portal, or profile creation.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: The Application contains no third-party telemetry, crash reporting services, or advertising networks that collect or share your device status or behavior.

3. Local Device Operations

Any settings or diagnostic configurations used within the Application are stored entirely on your local device. These files and parameters are fully contained within the Application's private sandbox and are never uploaded to remote servers or cloud systems.

4. Permissions Usage

The Application may request certain system permissions purely to facilitate local diagnostic or control features. These permissions are used exclusively to execute actions locally under your direct supervision and control. No permission requested by the Application is used to transmit data off your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate with any external third-party services or cloud APIs that track or monitor user actions. There are no background trackers or external web services linked to this software.

6. Children's Privacy

Because the Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information of any kind, it is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations worldwide, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be made available directly within the updated application or via our repository. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our local-only data approach.
